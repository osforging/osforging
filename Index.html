This is a website all about various aspects of forging and how to get started doing it. (PS. This is all propane forging. I have no experience with coal)

Supplies:

To get started forging you don't need very many tools. All you really need is a source of heat, a hard flat surface to pound on, and a hammer. If you have a gravel lot or some area in your yard that you don't care about being burned, that is best for an area to get started.

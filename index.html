This is a website all about various aspects of forging and how to get started doing it. (PS. This is all propane forging. I have no experience with coal)

Supplies:

To get started forging you don't need very many tools. All you really need is a source of heat, a hard flat surface to pound on, and a hammer. If you have a gravel lot or some area in your yard that you don't care about being burned, that is best for an area to get started. As for the hard flat surface, all you need is something big and flat that is steel. Cast iron (like the cast iron anvils at Harbor Freight will work, but they are very soft and I don't reccomend it.... (continued on the supplies page)
